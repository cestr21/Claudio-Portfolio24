Who I am

I am Claudio Estrada, born and raised in Amarillo, TX, with a rich cultural heritage stemming from my Mexican roots in Guadalajara, Jalisco. Growing up, my love for sports, especially soccer, and later boxing, shaped my competitive spirit.

When not in the ring, I channel my creativity into various pursuits, from sketching to carpentry and photography. Fueled by a lifelong fascination with technology, I discovered the power of creation within cyberspace. This passion led me to pursue a career in tech, where I now thrive as a frontend engineer. Blending my diverse interests with precision coding, I craft immersive digital experiences that reflect both my heritage and my commitment to excellence.

Punching up code and design.

From the ring to the web: delivering knockout websites.
